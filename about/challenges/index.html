Online Challenges

For the past couple of years, Exothermic Robotics teams have participated in various Online Challenges, from the Electronics Online Challenge sponsored by Texas Instruments to the Team Website Challenge sponsored by Dell. These Online Challenges provide a fun and engaging way to test as well as learn new STEM-based skills. This year, considering the increased need for virtual learning opportunities due to recent events, the Robotics Education & Competition (REC) Foundation has expanded its online challenges, and as a club, we are extremely excited to participate in these events!
