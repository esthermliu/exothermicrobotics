Exothermic Teams

At Exothermic Robotics, we have 7 highly capable teams. Each team has about 5 to 7 people, who are all equally ambitious about robotics. All the teams have robots unique to their team. Exothermic teams are all student run, without any parent help, demonstrating how much time and energy they all spend designing, building, and programming their robots.
