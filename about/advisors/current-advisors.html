Current Advisors

All of our current advisors/mentors are Exothermic Robotics alumni. Although they have already graduated, they continue to volunteer their time in helping current EXO members. As such, we would like to recognize and give them a huge shoutout for all their support and guidance!

Build Mentor

Vishwa KN

Vishwa was a member of team 10E from the 2016 Starstruck season until the 2020 Tower Takeover season. In the 2019-2020 season, he served as the Exothermic Robotics President. Vishwa is currently a build mentor for EXO.

Programming Mentor

Thorsten Lannynd

Thorsten was a member of team 10E from the 2017 In the Zone season until the 2020 Tower Takeover season. From 2018 till 2020, he was the Exothermic Robotics Programming Advisor. Thorsten is currently a programming mentor for Exo.

Programming Mentor

Tony Li

Tony was a member of Exothermic Robotics from the 2016 Starstruck season until the 2020 Tower Takeover season and competed on various Exo teams throughout the seasons. In his years at Exo, he has served as the Programming Advisor and Parts Manager. Tony is currently a programming mentor for Exo.
