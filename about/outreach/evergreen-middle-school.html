Outreach

Evergreen Middle School

Evergreen Middle School

Helena Zheng (member of team 10C) volunteered every week for the Evergreen Middle School robotics club, which has 18 middle school Vex IQ teams. She helped teach the teams about the basics of robotics such as building and programming, as well as online challenges. When Evergreen Middle School's robotics club hosted a live remote skills-only competition on January 17th, Helena took on the role of running tournament manager and being a referee in the remote setting.
