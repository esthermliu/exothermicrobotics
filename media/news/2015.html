April 8

10Q VEX Skyrise Worlds Reveal

10Q reveals their robot for the VEX Worlds Championship. It has a 12 point auton and a 33 Point Skills. We wish 10Q good luck at the competition!

March 24

Redmond Exothermic Robotics Teams Qualify for VEX World Championship

Three Exothermic Robotics Teams have qualified and will be attending the 2015 VEX Robotics World Championship in Lousville, Kentucky in April. The teams won 3 of the 5 spots that would allow them to move on. The following teams will be flying to Kentucky for the championship: Team 575, Team 10X, and Team 10Q.
