April 14

Local Team Creates Winning Robots

Engineering Explorer Post 2036, nicknamed "Exothermic Robotics Club", has members from both Redmond High School and International Community School (ICS). The Post sent three teams to the FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) robotics tournament that took place last month. Team 418 excelled with their robot "Chopstix", and won the Inspire Award, qualifying for a position at the World Championships in Atlanta on April 16-19.
